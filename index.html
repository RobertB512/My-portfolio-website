Christmas Advent Calendar

It's probably not anywhere near Christmas, but it was getting closer when I started making this site. This is just a simple Christmas advent calendar. Each day starting December 1st, and going through December 25th you can get a little something each day by pressing a button. You'll have to check the site out to see what you'll get.

IMPORTANT: If it's not December and you want to access the content for each day of the calendar, tap or click the "v" in the "Christmas Advent" heading 5 times.
